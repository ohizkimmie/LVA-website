Three of Cups
○

Three of Cups

UprightFriendship, celebration, community, joy, reunion, togetherness, sisterhood, abundance
ReversedOverindulgence, gossip, isolation, falling out, exclusion, cancelled plans, toxic group dynamics

Narrative Meaning

The Three of Cups is the group chat that actually makes you feel good. Three figures raise their cups to the sky in pure celebration — flowers in their hair, abundance at their feet, nothing but joy in the air. This is the card of your people. The ones you call when something amazing happens, the ones who show up when something doesn't. It's pure community energy — the reminder that joy shared is joy multiplied.

Practical Note

Celebrate. Connect. Let yourself be held by your people and hold them back. If there's a gathering, a reunion, or a reason to raise a glass — this card is giving you full permission to enjoy it completely.
Celebrate with your people — joy shared is joy multiplied.
